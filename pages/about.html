ABOUT HER PROJECTS

Her photography touches on the poetic while striving to remain authentic and true at the same time. She aims to evoke sentiments by using and mixing different photographic approaches and analog cameras. The prime focus of Victoria’s work is on everyday life: family, friends, places that touch her.
